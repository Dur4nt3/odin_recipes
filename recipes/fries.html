Cheese Fries Recipe:



Ingredients:

  • Frozen Steak Fries
  • Cheddar Cheese Soup Milk
  • 2% Milk Milk
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Paprika

Directions:

  1. Bake fries at 450F for 15-18 minutes or until golden brown

  2. Combine cheese, milk, garlic and onion powder in a sauce pen, and heat the mixture over medium heat

  3. Once the fries are done, drizzle the cheese over the fries
